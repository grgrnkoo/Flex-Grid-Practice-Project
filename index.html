About Us

Brand was founded in 2018 by one person. We started as a BMX community in VK social media, then we felt a lack of quality parts on our market, so the founder, studying at engineering university, decided to try doing few by his own technical designs. Since then we are producing few capsule collections a year for our community.

Main goal stays the same:

To do a parts, which no one does!

What is our goal?

Since we started a community, our main goal was to gather all community and teach them to love each other, their bikes and spots they ride. Love is the key to success, so we always tried to produce something unique in our country> Something that started to get popular in US, but still didn't get to our motherland. We sincerelly hope that using our parts makes people happy! Especially in the moments when it does the job, that it's been created for.

Just love and respect each other and ride bikes!
